SPACE MAPPINGS ?Space mappings connect rooms in your Matterport tour to your agent. Each space needs a name and a Sweep ID. The agent uses these to physically navigate visitors to the right location inside the tour while talking to them.

Map your spaces.

Connect your Matterport spaces so your agent can navigate visitors through your tour.

  • No spaces configured
  1. Open your Matterport tour in a browser
  2. Navigate to the room you want to map
  3. Copy the URL from the address bar
  4. Paste it here — we'll extract the Sweep ID automatically

The Sweep ID is the ss= parameter in the Matterport URL, e.g. ?m=...&ss=abc123

For advanced mapping with Matterport tour preview, use the full Space Mapper tool.
